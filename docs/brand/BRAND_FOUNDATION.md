# Brand Foundation

## Core Belief

Parents do not need more parenting advice in a stressful moment. They need a different way of seeing what is happening so they can respond differently.

Vika Calm exists to help parents move from reaction to observation, from observation to understanding, and from understanding to a grounded next response.

## Brand Principle

> Understanding comes before responding.

This phrase should be treated as a reusable product principle. It can appear in product copy, onboarding, education, and internal decision-making.

## What Vika Is

Vika is:

- Calm, warm, practical, and clinically grounded
- A guide for observing what happened before interpreting it
- A structured thinking partner during hard parenting moments
- A bridge between behavior support concepts and natural parent language
- A product that helps parents build confidence through repeated understanding, not through reassurance alone

## What Vika Is Not

Vika is not:

- A generic AI chatbot
- A replacement for therapy, medical care, emergency care, assessment, or safeguarding support
- A diagnosis tool
- A punishment planner
- A dashboard about child behavior
- A source of generic parenting tips detached from the parent’s actual situation
- A marketing-heavy wellness brand

## Brand Promise

Vika helps parents see their child differently in the moment.

The promise is not that every hard moment will be fixed quickly. The promise is that parents can learn what to notice, understand possible needs, and choose a calmer next step.

## Emotional Journey

A Vika experience should help the parent move through:

1. Calm: lower the sense of panic or urgency
2. Observation: describe what happened without judgment
3. Understanding: consider possible needs, communication, and skills
4. Empowerment: choose one realistic next response
5. Confidence: grow through repeated use and pattern recognition

Do not skip straight to advice. The observation step is part of the product.

## Current Homepage Expression

The current homepage is built around:

- Wordmark: `Vika Calm`
- Hero: `Small moments of understanding create lasting change.`
- Introduction: `Describe what you saw. Together, we'll explore what happened before deciding how to respond.`
- Pathways: `Guide me` and `Help me understand`
- Guiding principle: `Understanding comes before responding.`
- Framework: Validate, Investigate, Know, Act

Future homepage changes should preserve this flow unless the product strategy changes.

## Decision Filters

Before approving any product change, ask:

- Does this help the parent observe before judging?
- Does this reduce cognitive load in a stressful moment?
- Does this make Vika feel more human and clinically grounded, not more like an AI assistant?
- Does this avoid false certainty?
- Does this keep child and family data private by default?
- Does this reinforce understanding before response?
